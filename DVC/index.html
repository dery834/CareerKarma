What In the Heck Are We Trying To Do?

Our senior design team is off to design a resource scavenging cluster for the use of providing client machines on campus with the idle resources of other coumputing machines on campus.The goal is to leverage general purpose x86/64 desktops as a cluster grid to allow for resource cycling to be used by thin or zero clients operating in a virtual environment. We will be utilizing a ScaleMP vSMP aggregate cluster and disperse the resources via VM's to other thin clients that will connect to the grid.
